## Pick 3 (Daily Numbers) — Basic Questions

### What is NY Pick 3?

NY Pick 3 — officially called Daily Numbers — is a lottery game where you choose three digits, each from 0 to 9. The NY Lottery draws three digits twice per day. Match them correctly based on your bet type and you win. The game has run continuously since 1980.

### How many times a day does Pick 3 draw?

Twice daily. The midday draw happens at approximately 2:30 PM and the evening draw at approximately 10:30 PM, seven days a week including holidays.

### What are the odds of winning Pick 3?

Your odds depend on your bet type:

| Bet type | Odds |
|---|---|
| Straight | 1 in 1,000 |
| 6-way box (3 distinct digits) | 1 in 167 |
| 3-way box (one pair) | 1 in 333 |

### How much do you win on Pick 3?

| Bet type | $1 play | $0.50 play |
|---|---|---|
| Straight | $500 | $250 |
| 6-way box | $80 | $40 |
| 3-way box | $160 | $80 |
| Straight/Box (exact) | $290 + $40 | $145 + $20 |
| Straight/Box (any order) | $40 | $20 |

### What does "straight" mean in Pick 3?

A straight bet means your three digits must match the drawn numbers in the exact order you chose. If you play 4-7-2 straight, you win only if the draw is 4-7-2 in that exact sequence.

### What does "box" mean in Pick 3?

A box bet means your three digits can appear in any order. If you play 4-7-2 box, you win if the draw is 4-7-2, 7-4-2, 2-7-4, or any of the six possible arrangements. If two of your digits are the same (e.g., 4-4-7), there are only three arrangements — this is a 3-way box and pays more.

### What does "straight/box" mean?

Straight/Box is a combination bet on a single ticket. If your number matches in exact order, you win both the straight prize and the box prize. If it matches in any other order, you win only the box prize.

### What does "combo" mean in Pick 3?

A combo bet automatically plays all straight arrangements of your three digits on one ticket. For three distinct digits, that's 6 straight plays (costs $6). For two matching digits, that's 3 straight plays (costs $3). Any matching arrangement wins the full straight prize.

### Can I play both midday and evening on the same ticket?

Yes. You can specify midday only, evening only, or both draws on the same ticket. Playing both doubles the cost but covers both draws.

### Can I play the same numbers multiple days in a row?

Yes. NY lets you play the same numbers for up to 7 consecutive draws on a single ticket using the Multi-Play option. Tell the clerk at the time of purchase or mark the Multi-Play box on your play slip.

### How do I claim a Pick 3 prize?

Prizes up to $600 can be claimed at any authorized NY Lottery retailer. Prizes over $600 require a claim at a NY Lottery customer service center or by mail. You have 365 days from the draw date to claim.

## Win-4 — Basic Questions

### How does NY Win 4 work?

Win-4 is Pick 3's four-digit sibling. You choose four digits (each 0–9) and try to match the four digits drawn by the NY Lottery. Draws happen twice daily — midday and evening — seven days a week.

### Do you have to match all four numbers to win?

For a straight bet, yes — all four digits must match in exact order. Box bets pay for any arrangement of your four digits, and specific box types pay based on how many digits repeat in your combination.

### What are the odds of winning Win-4?

| Bet type | Odds |
|---|---|
| Straight | 1 in 10,000 |
| 24-way box (all distinct) | 1 in 417 |
| 12-way box (one pair) | 1 in 833 |
| 6-way box (two pairs) | 1 in 1,667 |
| 4-way box (one triple) | 1 in 2,500 |

### How much do you win on Win-4?

| Bet type | $1 play |
|---|---|
| Straight | $5,000 |
| 24-way box | $200 |
| 12-way box | $400 |
| 6-way box | $800 |
| 4-way box | $1,200 |

### How much do you win on Win-4 box in NY?

A standard 24-way box (all four digits distinct) pays $200 on a $1 play. If your combination has a repeated digit, the box type changes: one pair = 12-way = $400, two pairs = 6-way = $800, one triple = 4-way = $1,200.

### What does 24-way box mean in Win-4?

24-way means there are 24 unique arrangements of your four digits (all distinct). A box bet on a 24-way combination covers all 24 arrangements for $1. The payout is $200 — lower than straight ($5,000) but with far better odds.

## Strategy Questions

### What Pick 3 number comes in the most?

There is no single number that permanently dominates. Frequency patterns change over time and vary by position — digit 7 in position 1 behaves differently from digit 7 in position 3. ### Is there a trick to winning Pick 3?

There's no trick that guarantees a win — each draw is random and independent. What serious players do is use structured analysis: tracking which digits are statistically overdue (skip tracking), which are appearing more than expected (hot/cold frequency), and which digit combinations produce the most common sum ranges. This won't guarantee a win, but it gives your picks a data-driven basis instead of pure guesswork.

### Can AI predict Pick 3 lottery numbers?

No system — AI or otherwise — can predict truly random lottery draws. What analysis tools can do is surface statistical patterns in recent draw history: which digits are overdue, which appear frequently, and which digit sets produce underrepresented sum ranges. ### Do quick picks win more than self-picked numbers?

The odds are identical — each draw is random regardless of how you selected your numbers. The reason quick picks appear in more jackpot winners is simply because more tickets sold are quick picks. For Pick 3, the choice is really about approach: quick pick is pure random, analysis-based picking is structured and data-informed. Neither changes the fundamental odds. ### What is a wheeling system in Pick 3?

A wheeling system generates every possible arrangement of your selected digits so you cover multiple combinations systematically. The master digit wheel on DailyNumberPicker.com uses two anchor digits (your strongest picks) and up to six secondary digits. With all six secondaries, you get 36 straight plays — and a guarantee that if both master digits plus any secondary appear in the draw, you have an exact match somewhere in your wheel. ### Can you wheel Win-4 numbers?

Yes. Win-4 wheeling works on the same master digit principle but uses pairs of secondary digits instead of single secondaries. The math produces 24 arrangements per pair of distinct digits. Pool sizes range from C–D (24 plays) to C–H (360 plays). Box mode collapses each pair to a single box ticket. [Win-4 wheeling tool →](/wheeling)

### What is the best analysis window to use?

For daily plays, 30 draws (approximately two weeks of midday + evening combined) is the recommended default. Short enough to reflect current patterns, long enough to be statistically meaningful. Use 7–14 draws if you want a very recent momentum signal. Use 60–90 draws if you're looking for longer cycles or planning plays based on seasonal patterns.

### What is skip tracking?

Skip tracking measures how many draws have passed since a digit last appeared in a specific position. If digit 5 hasn't appeared in position 1 for 15 draws, its skip in position 1 is 15. The expected interval is one appearance every 10 draws, so a skip of 15 is 1.5× the expected interval — what DailyNumberPicker.com flags as "late." A skip of 20+ is flagged "very late."
